RestauRANTS

My Favorite Restaurants in Malmö

Hummusson

Healthy, vegan and delicious. Best Middle Eastern food in town.

Surf Shack

Huge hamburgers and garlic fries out of this world.

Domino's

Perhaps the only pizza that I like 100%. I mean, it's from back home so...

Delhi

Best Indian food. HUGE portions. Always have food left for the next day.

My Least Favorite Restaurants in Malmö

Casual

Expensive, mediocre and overrated burgers. Go to McDonald's instead.

Maya Cantina

As a Mexican, I think their food is good but EXTREMELY EXPENSIVE and the portion sizes are ridiculously small. They're the only Mexican restaurant in town, so they're very popular but I prefer to cook Mexican food at home.
